Why This Platform?

Phishing emails are designed to steal sensitive information like passwords, banking details, and personal data. Many users unknowingly fall victim to such attacks every day.

This system helps users analyze email content in real time and identify potential phishing attempts before any damage is done.

How It Works

  • Paste the email content into the scanner
  • System analyzes the content using security rules
  • A phishing risk score is calculated
  • Email is marked as SAFE or PHISHING

Key Features

  • Real-time phishing detection
  • Instant risk score generation
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Secure backend with database storage

Who Can Use This?

This tool is useful for students, professionals, small businesses, and anyone who wants to stay protected from email-based cyber threats.
